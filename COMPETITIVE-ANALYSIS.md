## DIRECT COMPETITORS

### 1. Sri Mandir (by AppsForBharat) — MARKET LEADER

| Attribute | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| Founded | ~2020, by Prashant Sachan |
| Downloads | **4 Crore+ (40 million)** |
| MAU | 3.5 million |
| Revenue | **$12 million+** annual run rate |
| Funding | **$50 million** (Series C — Susquehanna, Fundamentum, Elevation, Peak XV) |
| Team | ~250 employees |
| Diaspora Users | 700K registered, 90K MAU outside India |

**Features:** Online puja at 1000+ temples, chadhava offerings, prasad delivery, live darshan, devotional content (aarti, bhajan, chalisa), free horoscope & panchang, AI-powered spiritual assistant, multi-language.

**Revenue Model:** Commission on puja bookings, chadhava fees, prasad delivery charges.

**Diaspora ARPU:** Rs 7,000 ($81) internationally vs Rs 600-800 ($7-9) in India — **10x higher**.

| Strengths | Weaknesses |
|-----------|-----------|
| Dominant market position (40M downloads) | **No temple management/ERP tools (purely B2C)** |
| Strong VC backing ($50M) | **Temples don't control their own operations** |
| AI integration | Revenue still modest relative to user base |
| Growing diaspora revenue | No community/social features |
| Plans for 20+ temple town investment | No queue/crowd management |

## GOVERNMENT PLATFORMS

### TTD (Tirumala Tirupati Devasthanams) — Gold Standard
- **Scale:** Richest & most-visited Hindu temple in the world
- **Digital:** Special entry darshan booking, accommodation, seva booking, hundi donations, live streaming, OTP login, multi-language
- **Sets the benchmark** for temple digitization

### Tamil Nadu HR&CE — ITMS
- **Scale:** **44,300+ temples** under management
- **Digital:** Bilingual websites, online services, geo-location apps, QR verification, prasad delivery via Speed Post (48 temples)
- **Opportunity:** Most of 44K temples are small/rural with minimal digital capability — **huge gap for Soruban to fill**

### Kashi Vishwanath / Sugam Darshan
- Online VIP darshan (Rs 300-1000), time slots, QR e-tickets, free locker, prasad box included

### Ayodhya Ram Mandir
- Free Sugam Darshan e-pass, 10,000 CCTV cameras, AI drones, facial recognition, ANPR systems, virtual/metaverse darshan

## SWOT ANALYSIS — Soruban Temple

### Strengths
- **Unified B2B + B2C** — no competitor does both well
- **QR archana sheet** — unique innovation
- **Tamil Nadu focus** — deep understanding of local temple culture
- **Temple-first approach** — temples control their operations (builds trust)
- **Full ecosystem** — puja + priest + astrologer + community + e-commerce

### Weaknesses
- **New entrant** — no brand recognition yet
- **Bootstrapped/early stage** — limited marketing budget
- **Sri Mandir has 40M+ downloads** — massive head start in B2C
- **Temple onboarding is high-touch** — requires ground team

### Opportunities
- **44,300+ TN temples** largely un-digitized
- **Government push** for temple digitization (ITMS, Smart Temple Mission)
- **Diaspora segment** (10x ARPU)
- **Post-COVID permanent shift** to digital spiritual services
- **Astrology market convergence** ($100M+ opportunity)
- **Small temples** completely ignored by competitors

### Threats
- **Sri Mandir** could add B2B tools (they have the funding)
- **Astrotalk** could expand into temple services
- **Government platforms** (HR&CE ITMS) could improve and crowd out private players
- **Temple resistance** to digital adoption (traditional mindset)
- **Payment trust issues** for religious transactions online

---

## KEY STRATEGIC RECOMMENDATIONS

1. **Start hyper-local in Tamil Nadu** — own the TN market before expanding. 44K temples is a moat.
2. **Temple-first onboarding** — give temples free management tools, charge only on transactions. Remove friction.
3. **QR archana as the hook** — this single feature can drive temple adoption and devotee downloads simultaneously.
4. **Build for diaspora from Day 1** — NRI Tamil devotees will pay 10x and help fund growth.
5. **Integrate astrology early** — the Rs 659 Cr Astrotalk market proves willingness to pay. Don't leave this to competitors.
6. **Priest marketplace is a moat** — building a verified, rated priest network is hard to replicate and creates lock-in.
7. **Partner with HR&CE** rather than compete — offer to digitize their 44K temples as an authorized tech partner.
8. **Content creates retention** — daily panchang, slokas, festival content keeps users opening the app even when not booking.
